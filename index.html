Group history

Limp Bizkit was founded in the mid-90s in Florida. It includes vocalist Fred Durst, bass player Sam Rivers, drummer John Otto, DJ DJ Lethal and colorful guitarist Wes Borland. Over its long creative life, the group was nominated for three Grammy awards, received many other awards and awards, released 7 successful albums and sold more than 100 million copies of their recordings.
